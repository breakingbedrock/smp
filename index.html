Who we are Breaking Bedrock SMP is a Server where everyone strives to improve and to be self-sufficient. We look for people who want to build big builds and be active members of our community. Breaking Bedrock is an ongoing long-term server that has been active for about 4 years now, so keep that thought in mind when considering joining. Above all, we look for someone who fits in well with the community, strives for greatness, is mature, and follows the rules accordingly. If you get accepted there will be a two-week probation period where you will be a recruit. Use this time to show us what you got. At the end of the two weeks, you will either become a full member, be extended a week, or be asked to leave.
