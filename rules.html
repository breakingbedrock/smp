Breaking Bedrock
 Rules

1. No griefing players (Stealing, killing, or destroying anything you did not build.)

2. No building, destroying, or resource gathering or mining in or around the Shopping District without permission.

3. Do not settle near the shopping district (At least 1k blocks away.)

4. PVP is only for events or wars, no killing. During a war or event, no bases are to be damaged unless specified.

5. Only build within your land claim

6. No trespassing on other people’s bases or walled-off property even if they invited you once still ask before going there again. If you violate this rule, you can be kicked, suspended, or banned. (This includes not using others’ farms without permission)

7. This is a Private Server do not give out the connection info, invite anyone or add alternate accounts. Let a council member know and they will start the joining/camera account approval process.

8. Streaming and Videos are allowed, however, if you include anyone’s builds you have to ask before you record there. Also, you have to post the link to the videos on the discord so that we can all see them.

9. No Duplicating at all through any means whether it be mods or glitches this will result in an instant ban.

10. No exploiting any glitches at all. These include but are not limited to; XP, Duping, Farming, TNT, etc. (0-tick Kelp farms are allowed)

11. Resource packs can only be used to change the looks of something, any X-Ray, or exploitive UI changes are not allowed. Breaking this rule will result in an immediate ban.

12. Please use the Discord channels for their intended uses(i.e. no meme wars).

13. There is a 5k block border on the server. You are free to explore outside the border. But we will be having a monthly pruning and cutting of the chunks outside there, so everything outside that border will be lost.

IF YOU BREAK ANY OF THESE RULES YOU COULD BE SUSPENDED OR BANNED FROM THE SERVER!!
